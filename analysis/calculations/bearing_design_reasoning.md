# Bearing Selection Reasoning
## Role of Bearings
Bearings were incorporated to support shafts in transmission, to minimise friction and ensure alignment between gears. The correct bearing support in critical to ensure efficient torque transmission and prevent shaft bending.
## Bearing Type Selection
Deep-groove ball bearings were chosen as they primarily support the radial loading expected due to the spur gears.

Advantages of deep-groove ball bearings include:
- Low rotational friction
- Compact geometry
- Readily available
- Simple mounting and maintenance

## Bearing Size Selection
Input Shaft - 608ZZ (8mm bore)

Intermediate Shaft - 6000 (10mm bore)

Output Shaft - 6001 (12mm bore)

Bearing sizes increase to suit the increasing shaft sizes and increasing torque transmitted.

## Bearing Mounting
Bearings will be press fit into housing and aligned to ensure minimal shaft bending and correct gear meshing.
